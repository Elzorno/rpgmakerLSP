# Coalmouth Region

Coalmouth Region is the first mainland region after Home Island.

It introduces a larger world, harder labor, industrial ruins, and the first strong hint that ancient “magic” can behave like broken automation.

---

## Purpose

Coalmouth should teach that the old world did not only leave behind shrines and swords. It left behind systems people still depend on.

This region introduces:

- first mainland town,
- mining culture,
- Vera,
- industrial control compromise,
- Relay Node Six,
- stronger monster variants,
- first clear contrast between folklore and practical engineering.

---

## Player-Facing Summary

A soot-stained mining region of cliffs, rails, lamps, old tunnels, stubborn workers, and machines that sometimes move when no one touches them.

People call the problem “mine spirits.” Vera is not so sure.

---

## Hidden Reality

Coalmouth is built over old mining, transit, and industrial control infrastructure.

The region’s crisis is not supernatural. It is a degraded automation and control-system failure influenced by the relay network.

---

## Encounter Philosophy

Enemies should become more industrial than Home Island:

- Ash Rat variants evolve toward Tunnel Rat / Wire Rat,
- Gels become Soot Gel or Coal Gel,
- early golem/construct enemies appear,
- mine scorpion or cave pest variants introduce status effects.
