# Golem Family

The Golem Family represents heavy guardian and labor-construct enemies.

In Coalmouth, golems should feel like mine equipment, old maintenance systems, and stone-and-metal labor machines misunderstood as animated earth.

---

## Purpose

Golems introduce tougher construct-style enemies and prepare the player for Node Six's industrial-control theme.

---

## Fantasy Presentation

Large stone or coal-bodied creatures that move with grinding weight and seem to obey mine spirits.

---

## Hidden Reality

Autonomous maintenance, hauling, or safety machines degraded into hostile patterns by corrupted control signals.

---

## Silhouette

- Large blocky body.
- Heavy arms.
- Short legs or base-like stance.
- Core light or furnace-like center.
- Mixed rock, coal, and old metal.

---

## Behavior Pattern

- high defense,
- slow speed,
- heavy attacks,
- may guard or charge.
