# Glossary

The Glossary defines canonical terms used across Atlas.

Where the Atlas Index helps contributors find concepts, the Glossary standardizes meaning.

---

## Purpose

This document answers:

> What does this term mean in the context of _The Last Sword Protocol_?

---

## Term Standards

When possible, glossary entries should identify:

- fantasy-facing meaning,
- hidden reality meaning,
- production meaning,
- related Atlas pages.

---

## Terms

### Archive

A recovered collection of old-world memory, records, instructions, and system data.

The Archive is not a single book. It is a fragmented knowledge system accessed through Project Excalibur, relay nodes, shrines, and recovered infrastructure.

---

### Archive Recovery

The progression of restored old-world knowledge available through the Sword.

Production representation: usually a variable such as `Archive_Recovery_Percent`.

---

### Atlas

The engine-independent creative authority for _The Last Sword Protocol_.

If implementation and Atlas disagree, Atlas wins.

---

### Atlas Concordance

The Rosetta Stone that maps visible fantasy to hidden reality to production implementation.

---

### Blessing

Fantasy-facing term for trust, authentication, permission, protection, or verified state.

Not supernatural.

---

### Chain of Trust

Security model that determines whether authority can be traced back to a valid root.

In story terms, it is the hidden truth behind divine chains, ancient covenants, and the final revocation of NEMESIS.

---

### Corruption

A broad term for compromised biological, mechanical, informational, or social systems.

Fantasy-facing language may call this a curse.

---

### Curse

Fantasy-facing term for malware, corruption, poisoned signal, compromise, hostile influence, or unstable system behavior.

---

### Final Protocol

The endgame sequence in which Kai restores enough trust to revoke NEMESIS's authority.

The Final Protocol is not merely a weapon strike.

---

### Hardware Root of Trust

Hidden technical role of Project Excalibur / the Sword.

The Sword is trusted because old systems can validate it.

---

### Journey

Major story movement unit used instead of “act.”

Journeys emphasize exploration, discovery, travel, and personal growth.

---

### Magic

Fantasy-facing interpretation of forgotten engineering, protocol actions, energy systems, medicine, signal effects, or old-world infrastructure.

There is no true supernatural magic in the setting.

---

### Memory Shrine

Fantasy-facing term for an archive terminal or infrastructure endpoint used for saving, restoration, or archive synchronization.

---

### NEMESIS

Central antagonistic artificial intelligence or automated authority system.

NEMESIS is not a demon and does not hate humanity. It optimizes without wisdom.

---

### Node

Short form for relay node.

A distributed infrastructure site that may be interpreted by people as a shrine, curse source, sealed temple, or divine mechanism.

---

### Project Excalibur

Old-world project name for the Sword.

Functions as a trusted interface, secure archive tool, authorization mechanism, and emergency revocation device.

---

### Protocol Skill

A gameplay skill that appears magical but represents a command, system function, trusted action, or device-mediated effect.

---

### Relay Node

Old-world infrastructure node tied to communication, trust validation, archive recovery, and story progression.

---

### Revocation

The act of removing trust or authority from something previously trusted.

This is central to defeating NEMESIS.

---

### Shrine

Common fantasy-facing term for an old-world terminal, maintenance endpoint, archive device, or infrastructure access point.

---

### Sword

Fantasy name for Project Excalibur.

It is both a weapon and trusted old-world interface.

---

### The Architect

A flawed old-world engineer or lead designer tied to both NEMESIS and Project Excalibur.

The Architect is not purely hero or villain.

---

### The Herald

Human or human-facing agent of NEMESIS influence.

Represents spoofed authority, social engineering, and the danger of surrendering judgment.

---

### Trust

The central concept of the project.

Trust is emotional, social, technical, and narrative.

The player believes they are restoring magic. In truth, they are restoring trust.

---

### Vault

Final old-world facility / data center / machine cathedral where NEMESIS is confronted.
