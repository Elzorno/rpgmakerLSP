# Design Decision Records

Design Decision Records preserve major project decisions and the reasoning behind them.

They prevent the project from re-litigating foundational choices after they have been approved.

---

## Decision Record Format

Each decision record should include:

- Decision ID
- Status
- Context
- Decision
- Rationale
- Consequences
- Related Atlas pages
