# Coalmouth Battler Prompt Packet

This packet defines first-pass image-generation prompts for Coalmouth enemy battlers.

All assets should be RPG Maker MZ-compatible front-view battlers with transparent backgrounds.

---

## Global Style Requirements

```text
front-view RPG Maker MZ battler, transparent background, readable silhouette, original classic JRPG-inspired monster design, no text, no UI, no background, game asset ready
```

Avoid direct imitation of existing protected monster designs.

---

## MON-GEL-003 — Soot Gel

```text
Create a front-view RPG Maker MZ battler of an original gel monster called Soot Gel. It shares the same rounded family silhouette as earlier gel creatures, with a dark gray-black soot palette, tiny amber glow flecks inside the body, soft smoky edges, charming but dirtier Coalmouth mine personality, transparent background, no text, no UI, no background.
```

---

## MON-GLM-001 — Coal Golem

```text
Create a front-view RPG Maker MZ battler of an original enemy called Coal Golem. It is a chunky coal-and-stone maintenance construct with heavy arms, old metal braces, warm amber cracks like furnace light, slow defensive posture, readable classic JRPG silhouette, transparent background, no text, no UI, no background.
```

---

## MON-SCP-001 — Mine Scorpion

```text
Create a front-view RPG Maker MZ battler of an original monster called Mine Scorpion. It is a stylized cave scorpion with coal-black shell, amber highlights, large readable claws, curved raised tail, classic JRPG charm, not realistic horror, transparent background, no text, no UI, no background.
```

---

## BOS-N06-001 — Iron Foreman

```text
Create a front-view RPG Maker MZ boss battler of an original industrial mine guardian called Iron Foreman. It is made of iron plates, coal dust, chains, old signal lamps, and heavy tool-like arms, with an amber furnace glow and authoritative foreman silhouette. It should feel like broken labor automation, not a demon. Transparent background, no text, no UI, no background.
```

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- Each battler has transparent background.
- Soot Gel visually belongs to the Gel Family.
- Coal Golem feels heavy and slow.
- Mine Scorpion is readable but not too realistic.
- Iron Foreman communicates industrial control failure.
