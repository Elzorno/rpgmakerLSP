# Journey I — The Dreamer

Journey I is the opening movement of _The Last Sword Protocol_.

It begins in Ashford and ends when Kai shuts down Relay Node Seven, opening the path to the mainland.

---

## Purpose

Journey I teaches the player:

- who Kai is,
- what Ashford means,
- that exploration matters,
- that old technology exists but is misunderstood,
- that the Sword recognizes Kai,
- that Home Island hides Relay Node Seven.

---

## Core Question

> Who am I?

Kai does not answer this fully in Journey I. He only learns that the question is larger than he believed.

## Ending State

By the end of Journey I:

- Kai has the Sword.
- Node Seven is offline.
- Ashford has changed.
- Rustshore Docks can send Kai to the mainland.
- The player understands that the island's myths were protecting something real.
