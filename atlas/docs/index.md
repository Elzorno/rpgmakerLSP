# Project Atlas

**Atlas is the complete creative authority for _The Last Sword Protocol_.**

Atlas is engine independent. RPG Maker MZ is the first implementation target, not the definition of the project.

If implementation and Atlas disagree, Atlas wins.

---

## What Atlas Is

Atlas is the living blueprint for the world, story, gameplay, visual language, hidden cybersecurity layer, production standards, and AI workflow of _The Last Sword Protocol_.

It exists so that a future contributor — human or AI — can understand not only what to build, but why it belongs.

---

## Primary Rule

> The player believes they are restoring magic.
>
> In truth, they are restoring trust.

## Current Phase

Atlas v1.0 is in **Architecture Phase**.

This phase creates the structure, standards, and operating rules that all future design pages will follow.

---

## Creative North Star

_The Last Sword Protocol_ should feel like a classic Dragon Quest-inspired JRPG first: warm villages, strange ruins, charming monsters, secrets, exploration, hope, and emotional sincerity.

The hidden cybersecurity layer exists beneath the fantasy. It should make the world coherent without turning the game into a lecture.
