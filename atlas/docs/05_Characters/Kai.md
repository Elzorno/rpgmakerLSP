# Kai

Kai is the protagonist of _The Last Sword Protocol_ and the authenticated bearer of the Sword / Project Excalibur.

---

## Purpose

Kai anchors the player emotionally and mechanically.

He begins as an ordinary young person from Ashford and gradually becomes the person capable of restoring trust to the world.

---

## Role In Story

Kai inherits responsibility before understanding it.

Journey I should not make him feel like a fully formed hero. It should make him feel curious, uncertain, and brave enough to continue.

---

## Player-Facing Identity

A young villager from Ashford who discovers the legendary Sword beneath Skyreach Hill.

---

## Hidden-Layer Role

Kai is the identity-validated user of Project Excalibur.

He can satisfy the authentication requirements needed to interface with old-world relay and archive systems.

---

## Personality

- curious,
- sincere,
- observant,
- initially inexperienced,
- emotionally connected to home,
- grows into responsibility.

---

## Knowledge Level

Starts at Folk / Curious.

Progresses toward Technical Fragment and eventually True Technical understanding by late game.

---

## Character Arc

```text
Dreamer → Bearer → Investigator → Restorer → Guardian of the Future
```

---

## Gameplay Role

Balanced hero with access to Sword-linked Protocol Skills.
