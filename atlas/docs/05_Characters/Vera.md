# Vera

Vera is the practical engineer of the party and the first major companion introduced on the mainland.

---

## Purpose

Vera helps the player understand that the world’s “magic” has patterns, causes, and repeatable behavior.

She is not a modern engineer by vocabulary, but she thinks like one.

---

## Role In Story

Vera becomes central during the Coalmouth Mine Crisis.

She notices that the mine’s strange behavior follows rhythms, commands, and failures rather than superstition.

---

## Player-Facing Identity

A mechanic, repair worker, or tinkerer from Coalmouth who keeps old machinery working better than anyone else in town.

---

## Hidden-Layer Role

Vera intuitively understands degraded industrial control systems and old machinery behavior without knowing the old-world terminology.

---

## Personality

- practical,
- blunt when needed,
- observant,
- mechanically curious,
- protective of workers,
- skeptical of lazy mystical explanations,
- secretly hopeful that broken things can be fixed.

---

## Knowledge Level

Practical → Technical Fragment.

Vera should avoid modern jargon early but can describe actual engineering concepts in plain language.

---

## Character Arc

```text
Mechanic → Skeptic → Investigator → Builder → Restorer
```

---

## Gameplay Role

Support / tools / repair / physical combat hybrid.

Potential abilities:

- repair ally equipment or status,
- disrupt machines,
- expose enemy weaknesses,
- stabilize old mechanisms,
- use tool-based attacks.

---

## Dialogue Rules

Vera should speak plainly and practically.

She should challenge bad assumptions without mocking ordinary people for believing what their culture taught them.
