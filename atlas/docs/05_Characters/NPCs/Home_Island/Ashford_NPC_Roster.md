# Ashford NPC Roster

This roster defines the first-pass NPC set for Ashford.

Only Grandmother Elara is currently a full object. The remaining NPCs are role-based placeholders until they are named and promoted into permanent NPC objects.

---

## Purpose

Ashford NPCs should make the village feel alive before the adventure escalates.

Each NPC should have a gameplay purpose and a worldbuilding purpose.

---

## Canonical NPC

| ID | Name | Role |
|---|---|---|
| NPC-ELA-001 | Grandmother Elara | Kai's emotional anchor and warning keeper |

---

## Story State Coverage

Every major Ashford NPC does not need unique lines for every state, but should support at least:

```text
Intro
After Tremor
After Node Seven
```

Elara should support all major states defined in the Ashford Dialogue Packet.

---

## Promotion Rule

Promote a placeholder NPC into a full object when:

- they start or complete a quest,
- they recur outside Ashford,
- they have an emotional arc,
- they represent an important world concept,
- they require portrait or unique sprite production.
